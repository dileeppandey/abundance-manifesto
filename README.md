# The Abundance Manifesto

**Scarcity is a policy choice. We choose differently.**

We live in a world technically capable of providing clean energy, shelter, food, knowledge, and tools to everyone. Yet our economic and legal systems enforce artificial scarcity — through intellectual property enclosures, planned obsolescence, and platform monopolies — to protect the profits of a few.

This repository is a living movement: a manifesto, a curated directory of open alternatives to proprietary systems, and a growing community of people who have pledged to build toward abundance.

---

## The Five Principles

| # | Principle | In Practice |
|:--|:---|:---|
| I | **Radical Openness** | Source code, blueprints, AI models, and knowledge belong to everyone |
| II | **Design Global, Make Local** | Share designs freely; manufacture in your community |
| III | **Regenerative Circularity** | Build systems that restore the planet, not just consume it |
| IV | **Cooperative Stewardship** | Those who create value must govern value |
| V | **Access Over Ownership** | Shared use creates abundance; hoarding creates scarcity |
