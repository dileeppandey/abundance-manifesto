# Open Alternatives: Productivity

*Office software, file storage, project management, and publishing tools.*

The tools we use to work shape the tools we build. If the infrastructure of daily knowledge work is controlled by Microsoft and Google, the output of that work — documents, designs, data — is locked into their formats and their surveillance. This category maps alternatives that keep your work yours.

---

## Office Suites

### [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org)
**Replaces:** Microsoft Office  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

The most widely used open-source office suite. Writer (Word), Calc (Excel), Impress (PowerPoint), Draw, and Base. Fully cross-platform. Maintained by The Document Foundation, a nonprofit. Used by governments across Europe including Germany, France, and Italy as their standard office suite.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://www.libreoffice.org/community/get-involved/) · [Donate](https://www.libreoffice.org/donate/)

---

### [OnlyOffice](https://www.onlyoffice.com)
**Replaces:** Microsoft Office / Google Docs  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Open-source office suite with high compatibility with Microsoft formats. Offers self-hosted collaboration (multiple users editing simultaneously). Desktop apps are free; self-hosted server is open source.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://github.com/ONLYOFFICE)

## File Storage & Collaboration

### [Nextcloud](https://nextcloud.com)
**Replaces:** Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Self-hosted cloud storage, document collaboration, calendar, contacts, and video calls — all in one platform you control. Used by universities, hospitals, and government agencies as their private cloud. Nextcloud Hub integrates file storage, collaborative office editing, and communication.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://github.com/nextcloud) · [Find a hosted provider](https://nextcloud.com/providers/)

---

### [Cryptpad](https://cryptpad.org)
**Replaces:** Google Docs  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Collaborative document editing with end-to-end encryption — not even the server can read your documents. Includes documents, spreadsheets, presentations, kanban boards, code pads, and whiteboards. No account needed for anonymous collaboration.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://github.com/xwiki-labs/cryptpad) · [Use the flagship instance](https://cryptpad.fr)

## Project Management

### [Plane](https://plane.so)
**Replaces:** Jira, Linear, Asana  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Open-source project management with issues, cycles, modules, and analytics. Self-hostable. MIT licensed. Has a cloud version for teams that prefer not to self-host.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://github.com/makeplane/plane)

---

### [Taiga](https://taiga.io)
**Replaces:** Jira, Trello  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Open-source project management for agile teams. Kanban, Scrum, and issue tracking. Self-hostable under AGPL. Offers a free cloud tier.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://github.com/taigaio)

---

### [Gitea](https://gitea.io) / [Forgejo](https://forgejo.org)
**Replaces:** GitHub, Bitbucket  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Lightweight, self-hosted Git service. Forgejo is a community-owned fork of Gitea with a stronger governance commitment. Used by organizations that cannot or should not rely on Microsoft-owned GitHub for their source code.

**Get involved:** [Contribute to Forgejo](https://codeberg.org/forgejo/forgejo)

## Design & Publishing

### [Inkscape](https://inkscape.org)
**Replaces:** Adobe Illustrator  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Professional vector graphics editor. Full SVG support. Used by illustrators, designers, and engineers worldwide for everything from logos to circuit board layouts.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://inkscape.org/contribute/)

---

### [GIMP](https://www.gimp.org)
**Replaces:** Adobe Photoshop  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

The GNU Image Manipulation Program. Full-featured raster graphics editor. Supports scripting and automation. Been in active development since 1995.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://www.gimp.org/develop/)

---

### [Krita](https://krita.org)
**Replaces:** Adobe Photoshop (for illustration and painting)  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Professional digital painting application. Particularly strong for illustration, concept art, and comic creation. Developed by the KDE community, funded partly by user donations.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://krita.org/en/get-involved/) · [Donate](https://krita.org/en/support-us/donations/)

---

### [Scribus](https://www.scribus.net)
**Replaces:** Adobe InDesign  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Open-source desktop publishing software. Used to produce books, magazines, newsletters, and brochures. Professional-grade PDF export.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://wiki.scribus.net/canvas/Development)

---

### [Blender](https://www.blender.org)
**Replaces:** Adobe After Effects, Autodesk Maya, Cinema 4D  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Open-source 3D creation suite — modeling, rigging, animation, simulation, rendering, compositing, and video editing. Funded by the Blender Foundation and corporate sponsors (including major studios that use it in production). One of the strongest examples of open-source matching or exceeding proprietary tools.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://www.blender.org/get-involved/) · [Donate](https://fund.blender.org/)

## Notes & Knowledge Management

### [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) *(free, not fully open source)*
**Replaces:** Notion, Roam Research  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness (partial)  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Local-first, markdown-based knowledge management. All data stored as plain text files you own. Core app is free; sync is paid. Not fully open source, but included here because of its local-first, no-lock-in philosophy. A strong alternative to cloud-locked tools.

**Caveat:** The core is proprietary. For a fully open-source alternative, see [Logseq](https://logseq.com) or [Foam](https://foambubble.github.io/foam/).

---

### [Logseq](https://logseq.com)
**Replaces:** Notion, Roam Research  
**Principle:** [I] Radical Openness  
**Maturity:** Production-ready  

Open-source, local-first knowledge management. All data in plain markdown and org-mode files. Graph view, journals, tasks, and whiteboards. Fully open source under AGPL.

**Get involved:** [Contribute](https://github.com/logseq/logseq)
